Welcome Back, Butterflies

I love butterflies.
我喜歡蝴蝶。
I love the orange and black ones.
我喜歡橘色和黑色的蝴蝶。
In the fall, they fly south.
在秋天，牠們會往南飛。
They fly away from me.
牠們從我身邊飛走。
In the spring, they fly north.
在春天，牠們會往北飛。
They fly back to me.
牠們飛回我身邊。
They fly back to lay their eggs.
牠們飛回來產卵。
They need the right plants for their eggs.
牠們需要適合的植物來產卵。
I plant seeds in my yard.
我在我的院子裡種種子。
I water the seeds.
我幫種子澆水。
I watch the seeds sprout.
我看著種子發芽。
I watch the plants grow.
我看著植物長大。
My yard has the right plants now.
我的院子現在有適合的植物了。
Welcome back, butterflies!
歡迎回來，蝴蝶們！
