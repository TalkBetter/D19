Welcome back, butterflies!

Earthworms Help Plants Grow

Earthworms are small animals.
They build long tunnels underground.
The tunnels help water and air reach plant roots.
Earthworms make space in the soil.
Earthworm waste helps plants grow big and strong.
Earthworms are small, but they have a big job.

Earthworm Bodies

The body of an earthworm is made up of many parts.
Each part is shaped like a ring.
Each part can bend and stretch.
Earthworms move by stretching and pulling their bodies.
