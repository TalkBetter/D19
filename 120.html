Lesson 120 中英對照

英文問答＋中文翻譯｜手機閱讀版

藍色為英文答案，下方為中文翻譯。空格 _____ 可代入人名。

1How often do we have homework?

我們多久有一次功課？

We have homework two or three times a month.

我們一個月有兩、三次功課。

We have recording homework every class.

我們每堂課都有錄音功課。

2What are you afraid of?

你害怕什麼？

I’m afraid of ghosts.

我害怕鬼。

I’m afraid of heights.

我怕高。

3Does your social studies teacher ever tell you stories about history?

你的社會課老師有時候會告訴你有關歷史的故事嗎？

Yes, he does. He often tells us stories about history. They’re interesting.

是的，他會。他常常告訴我們歷史故事。那些故事很有趣。

No, she doesn’t. She seldom tells us stories about history.

不，她不會。她很少告訴我們歷史故事。

4Do your parents ever take you to a restaurant?

你的父母有時候會帶你去餐廳嗎？

Yes, they do. They often take me to a restaurant.

是的，他們會。他們常常帶我去餐廳。

No, they don’t. They never take me to one.

不，他們不會。他們從來不帶我去餐廳。

5Are there any smart students in our class?

我們班裡有聰明的學生嗎？

Yes, there are. There are a lot of smart students in our class.

是的，有。我們班裡有很多聰明的學生。

6Is there an airport in Taipei?

台北有一個飛機場嗎？

Yes, there is. There’s one in Taipei.

是的，有。台北有一個。

Yes, there is. There’s an airport in Taipei.

是的，有。台北有一個機場。

7What does the boy like to talk about?

這個男孩喜歡談論什麼？

He likes to talk about video games.

他喜歡談論電玩遊戲。

He likes to talk about pretty girls.

他喜歡談論漂亮的女生。

8How do you get to school?

你如何到學校？

I walk to school.

我走路去學校。

My mother takes me to school on her scooter.

我媽媽騎機車載我去學校。

My father drives me to school.

我爸爸開車載我去學校。

9Does your father take the MRT to work?

你爸爸搭捷運上班嗎？

Yes, he does. He takes the MRT to work.

是的，他會。他搭捷運去上班。

No, he doesn’t. He doesn’t take it to work.

不，他不會。他不搭捷運去上班。

10How does he get to work?

他如何去上班？

He drives to work.

他開車去上班。

He gets to work by scooter.

他騎機車去上班。

He takes the MRT to work.

他搭捷運去上班。

11Who brings you to English class?

誰帶你來上英文課？

My mother brings me to English class.

我媽媽帶我來上英文課。

My father brings me here.

我爸爸帶我來這裡。

Nobody brings me here. I come by myself.

沒有人帶我來這裡。我自己來。

12Are you scared of ghosts?

你害怕鬼嗎？

Yes, I am. I’m scared of them.

是的，我怕。我害怕它們。

No, I’m not. I’m not scared of ghosts. I don’t believe in them.

不，我不怕。我不害怕鬼。我不相信有鬼。

13How many people are there in your family?

你的家庭裡有幾個人？

There are four people in my family.

我家有四個人。

14What do you usually take to school?

你通常帶什麼去學校？

I usually take my backpack, pencil case, and other things to school.

我通常帶書包、鉛筆盒和其他東西去學校。

15Do tests make you nervous?

考試會讓你緊張嗎？

Yes, they do. They make me very nervous.

是的，會。考試讓我很緊張。

No, they don’t. They don’t make me nervous.

不，不會。考試不會讓我緊張。

16Does our teacher ever tell you to pay attention?

我們的老師有時候會告訴你要專心嗎？

Yes, he does. He often tells me to pay attention.

是的，他會。他常常告訴我要專心。

No, he doesn’t. He never tells me to pay attention.

不，他不會。他從來沒有叫我要專心。

17Are there any elephants in Taipei Zoo?

台北動物園裡有大象嗎？

Yes, there are. There are a few elephants in Taipei Zoo.

是的，有。台北動物園裡有幾隻大象。

18Are you hungry?

你餓了嗎？

Yes, I am. I’m hungry.

是的，我餓了。

No, I’m not. I’m not hungry.

不，我不餓。

19How does my hair look?

我的頭髮看起來如何？

It looks beautiful.

它看起來很漂亮。

It looks nice.

它看起來很好看。

20Does _____ ever take your eraser without asking?

_____ 有時候會沒有經過你的同意就拿你的橡皮擦嗎？

Yes, he does. He often takes it without asking.

是的，他會。他常常沒有問就拿走它。

No, she doesn’t. She rarely takes my eraser without asking.

不，她不會。她很少沒有問就拿我的橡皮擦。

21Is your mother scared of bugs?

你媽媽害怕蟲嗎？

Yes, she is. She’s scared of bugs.

是的，她怕。她害怕蟲。

No, she isn’t. She isn’t scared of them.

不，她不怕。她不害怕牠們。

22Does _____ ever talk to animals?

_____ 有時候會和動物說話嗎？

Yes, he does. He sometimes talks to animals.

是的，他會。他有時候會和動物說話。

No, she doesn’t. She never talks to them.

不，她不會。她從來不和牠們說話。

23Is there an air conditioner in your bedroom?

你的臥室裡有一台冷氣機嗎？

Yes, there is. There’s an air conditioner in my bedroom.

是的，有。我的臥室裡有一台冷氣機。

No, there isn’t. There isn’t one in my bedroom.

不，沒有。我的臥室裡沒有冷氣機。

24Are there any sharks in the river?

河裡有鯊魚嗎？

No, there aren’t. There aren’t any sharks in the river.

不，沒有。河裡沒有鯊魚。

25Does _____ ever tell a lie?

_____ 有時候會說謊嗎？

Yes, he does. He sometimes tells a lie when he is too nervous.

是的，他會。他太緊張的時候有時候會說謊。

No, she doesn’t. She never tells a lie.

不，她不會。她從來不說謊。

26What does your teacher do when you make a mistake?

當你犯錯時，你的老師會做什麼？

He corrects me and makes me record when I make a mistake in class.

當我在課堂上犯錯時，他會糾正我，並讓我錄音。

He makes me keep standing when I make a mistake in class.

當我在課堂上犯錯時，他會讓我一直站著。

27How often does your father make dinner?

你爸爸多久做一次晚餐？

He makes dinner once a week.

他一週做一次晚餐。

He never makes dinner.

他從來不做晚餐。

28Does everybody have to answer these questions?

每個人都必須回答這些問題嗎？

Yes, we do. Everybody has to answer these questions.

是的，我們必須。每個人都必須回答這些問題。

Yes, we do. Everyone has to answer these questions.

是的，我們必須。每個人都必須回答這些問題。

29Is this homework as difficult as the last homework?

這次的功課和上次的功課一樣困難嗎？

Yes, it is. It’s as difficult as the last homework.

是的，一樣難。這次功課和上次功課一樣困難。

No, it isn’t. It isn’t as difficult as the last homework.

不，沒有。這次功課沒有上次功課那麼困難。
