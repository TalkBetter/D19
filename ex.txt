Mom likes watermelon _____ it is tasty.
答案：because
完整句：Mom likes watermelon because it is tasty.
Whitney has an ant queen _____ was black.
答案：which
完整句：Whitney has an ant queen which was black.
_____ Pam eat grapes?
答案：Does
完整句：Does Pam eat grapes?
I _____ like to try pancakes with ice cream.
答案：would
完整句：I would like to try pancakes with ice cream.
Look! There’s a butterfly flying _____ your head!
答案：above
完整句：Look! There’s a butterfly flying above your head!
